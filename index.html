WHO WE ARE

The Pur'N'Kleen [PNK] Organization is a resources and trading organization that aims to meet the galaxy's growing demand for energy in ways that are economically, environmentally and socially responsible. In other words, we make the best damn fuel out there and we have fun making it.

WHAT WE DO

Pur'N'Kleen is a global group of miners and harvesters within the Star Citizen Universe. Our main products are Pur'N'Kleen Boost Fuel, Pur'N'Kleen Quantum Fuel, and Pur'N'Kleen Racing Fuel. Whatever your engine and piloting environment, the Pur'N'Kleen brand is synonymous with extreme performance. As the organization grows, we also have plans to produce more Pur'N'Kleen products, such as Pur'N'Kleen Steel, Pur'N'Kleen Water, Pur'N'Kleen Pre-packaged Meals, and Pur'N'Kleen Meds.

In the Pur'N'Kleen Organization, the rules are simple. You own your ships and we don't tax you. Raw materials are pooled for bulk refining, quality control, and to optimize profits. Profits are distributed according to the amount of time contributed to the organization. There are no mandatory activities, except for initial training and bi-weekly check-ins to make sure you exist. If you mostly want to play alone, you will be able to do so as long as you contribute to the group.

At it's core, Pur'N'Kleen is a casual organization. We are all busy professionals who enjoy playing video games and dislike drama. If you just want a group of friends who play at regular times, you found the right place. If you want to take more responsibility such as becoming Fleet Commander, you will have the opportunity to do so.

BENEFITS

Financial Rewards and Benefits

PNK works to make you as much UEC as possible. You will make more money space trucking with us than going alone because we share resources and split expenses. Safety and Performance Bonuses are also available.

Training and Development

PNK Basic Training Program will ensure all members can perform all tasks safely and effectively before joining a Fleet activity. Members who wish to lead will be given the chance to do so. Ongoing development will be provided via the podcast.

Regular Fleet Events

You never have to fly alone. We will have at least 1 planned Fleet Event each weekend with more as our Organization grows in size. Each week, someone will be designated the Fleet Commander and he/she will be responsible for organizing our group play session.

Work/Life Balance

PNK caters to the busy professional. While we have organized activities, we will NEVER force you to attend them. We will ask that you check-in on the forums at least every two weeks. We will also ask that you complete the PNK Basic Training Program before participating in Fleet Events.

Diversity and Inclusion

PNK is a community of friends from all walks of life. We are committed to making sure everyone feels welcomed, respected and cared for. We will regularly conduct anonymous polls among members to identify any issues or areas for improvement.
