ABOUT US

Who are we?

The Pur'N'Kleen [PNK] Organization is a Star Citizen resources and trading organization that aims to meet the galaxy's growing demand for energy in ways that are economically, environmentally and socially responsible. In other words, we make the best damn fuel out there and we have fun making it.

What exactly do you do?

Our core business revolves around producing and selling our three main fuel products:

Pur'N'Kleen Boost Fuel
A high-performance boost fuel designed to provide the maximum possible acceleration for any engine.
Pur'N'Kleen Quantum Fuel
A quantum fuel product designed for a wide variety of operating conditions at an affordable price.
Pur'N'Kleen Racing Fuel
A racing fuel product designed to maximize performance in a wide range of race engine applications.

As membership grows, we plan to expand our Pur'N'Kleen product line with additional products, such as Pur'N'Kleen Steel, Pur'N'Kleen Water, Pur'N'Kleen Pre-packaged Meals, and Pur'N'Kleen Meds.

When was PNK founded?

The Pur'N'Kleen [PNK] Organization was officially created on December 2945, but much of it was developed many months prior.

Where is the Pur'N'Kleen HQ?

We are based out of the Nemo system.

Why Pur'N'Kleen?

Because The Expanse by James S.A. Corey is an awesome book and because we like making money.

Wait, I heard you split profits evenly among members?

Pur'N'Kleen works more like a tribe or family than a company. Members are equals part of the whole and they are expected to contribute and pull their weight, which is why we are VERY selective about who we bring in. All expenses and profits are shared among all members, with a certain amount reserved as bonuses for performance or safety. Yes. This means that inevitably some will put in more work than others, but we're all in this for fun, not ego.
